S.C.A.L.E.

Empowering Farms, One Algorithm at a Time!

Welcome to Smart Computation Algorithm for Livestock Estimation (S.C.A.L.E.)! Here's how to get started:
  1. Home Page: Start at the Home page to find different sections of the app.
  2. Capture Page: Go to the Capture page to see a live feed from the camera. Capture an image of your livestock. The app will detect the livestock and save their pictures.
  3. Results Page: Check the Results page to see the detected livestock. Click on any image to see its estimated weight. The app saves this information for you.
  4. Estimate Page: On the Estimate page, see the estimated weight of your livestock. You can delete any image if you want.
  5. Manage Page: Visit the Manage page to see a pie chart and tables with your livestock's information. Click on a livestock's name to see more details. You can close the details to go back to the Manage page or explore other parts of the app.
